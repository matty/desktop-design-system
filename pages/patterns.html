Resizable Split Panes

.ds-resizable, .ds-pane-first, .ds-pane-rest, and .ds-splitter[data-ds-splitter] create a draggable split layout. The ds:resize CustomEvent fires with detail.size on every resize. Add data-persist-key to name the split — persistence is the consuming app's responsibility. Add .is-horizontal to the wrapper for a top/bottom split.

Sidebar

Drag the divider. Double-click it to reset. Focus it and use arrow keys.

Content

This pane fills the remaining space.

Required: .ds-resizable wrapper, .ds-pane-first, .ds-splitter[data-ds-splitter], .ds-pane-rest. Optional: data-persist-key (the app listens for ds:resize and handles storage), .is-horizontal on the wrapper for row orientation. Clamping: min uses --ds-pane-min (160px), max uses --ds-pane-max (60%). Double-click the splitter to reset to default flex basis.
