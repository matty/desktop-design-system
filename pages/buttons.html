Desktop Design System

Buttons

One primary action per view (filled white). Everything else is a quiet secondary or ghost. Destructive actions use the danger style and usually a confirm dialog.

Variants

.ds-btn · .is-primary · .is-ghost · .is-danger · [disabled]

Sizes

.is-sm 28px · default 34px · .is-lg 40px

Icon buttons

.ds-btn.is-icon — square, no label, pair with title or a tooltip

Button group

For tightly-related actions that read as one control.

.ds-btn-group wrapping .ds-btns

In context — a primary action bar

Primary = the most likely next action for the current state; utilities collapse to icons on the right
