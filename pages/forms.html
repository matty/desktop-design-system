Filterable & multi-select combobox

Three combo modes: filterable single-select, multi-select with chips, and multi-select checklist.

.is-filterable — adds a filter <input> as first child of .ds-combo-menu; typing narrows options. .is-multi — multiple options can be selected; selected values render as removable .ds-chip elements inside the button. .is-multi.is-checklist — options show a checkmark when selected; the button shows "N selected" count instead of chips. All three modes keep the menu open while toggling; click outside or press Esc to close.
