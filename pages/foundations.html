Accent

Defaults to the OS system accent (AccentColor, with AccentColorText for ink). Brand it by overriding --accent and --accent-ink in your own stylesheet — every primitive recolors from the token.

--accentAccentColor
--accent-soft22% tint
--accent-border45% tint
Aa
--accent-inkAccentColorText

Accent marks primary actions and on/checked controls — primary buttons, toggles, checkboxes, radios, focus rings. Row and item selection is neutral grey (--selected), not accent, so colour always means "primary/on", never "highlighted".

Iconify is the catalog and Lucide is the default source, but output is offline static SVG from icons/registry.json. Use local semantic names from icons/approved.json.

Style: 24px viewBox, line icons, fill:none, stroke:currentColor, round caps and joins. Components size icons to 18px in navigation/buttons and 15px inline.

Motion

Short and quiet: --dur 140ms on --ease cubic-bezier(.2,.6,.2,1). The one ambient motion is the breathing status indicator.

Icons

Approved icons, rendered at build time from the offline registry via iconSvg() (the same helper shipped in the bundle).
