Drag-and-drop reordering

  • First item

  • Third item
[data-ds-sortable] on any list enables drag reordering via SortableJS (vendored). data-ds-sortable-handle=".ds-drag-handle" restricts dragging to the grip icon — text is not draggable. The drop target shows .ds-drop-placeholder (dashed var(--accent) border + var(--accent-soft) fill). The item being moved gets .is-dragging (raised shadow). Animation is zeroed when prefers-reduced-motion: reduce is active.

.ds-tree › .ds-tree-item › .ds-tree-row + .ds-tree-twisty + .ds-tree-label. Nest children in a <ul> inside .ds-tree-item. Leaf nodes (no child <ul>) are auto-detected and get .is-leaf; their twisty is hidden. Add .is-expanded to pre-open a node. Keyboard: Tab into the tree, Up/Down to move, Right to expand then descend, Left to collapse then move to parent, Home/End to jump, Enter/Space to select.
